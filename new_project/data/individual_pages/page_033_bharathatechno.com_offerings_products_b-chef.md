## B-Chef

#Online Ordering Platform for Home Chefs and Cloud Kitchens

#### Product Overview

B-CHEF is a custom web app designed for chefs, cafés, and food entrepreneurs to expand their digital presence. From cloud kitchens to personal brands, it enables direct customer connections, reduces reliance on third-party apps, and helps scale your business. As a dedicated food delivery software, B-CHEF makes your culinary offerings more visible, professional, and impactful.

### Tech Stack

##### Frontend

Next.js + TypeScript OR React.Js

##### Backend

Node.Js, Express.Js

##### Database

Mongo DB

##### Validation

Zod (Form validation)

##### Payments & Logistics

India: Razorpay, Global: Stripe, etc.

This stack allows the platform to load quickly, scale with order volume, and provide a smooth experience for both admin and end customers.

### Unique Selling Point

B-CHEF is a custom web app and standalone food delivery software that enables food businesses to showcase menus, take orders, process payments, and manage availability—all without relying on third-party apps or delivery services.

# Case Study: Nikita's Curry Corner

**Client:** Nikita's Curry Corner

**Industry:** Food & Beverage

**Use Case:** Tiffin service and home-chef food delivery software.

### Before the platform:

Nikita's Curry Corner was all about serving up delicious, home-cooked Sindhi meals to its loyal customers. However, the food business found itself heavily dependent on third-party apps like cookin.com, which not only charged steep commissions but also limited their control over branding and the overall customer experience. They faced challenges in customizing menus, connecting directly with customers, and managing their content due to the platform's restrictions. Plus, juggling external listings took their attention away from what they loved most—cooking. These hurdles made it tough to grow effectively and added financial strain as they tried to expand within the competitive cloud kitchen startup scene.

### After launching platform:

We launched a fully branded food ordering website with B-CHEF, a custom web app designed for food businesses. The menu could be updated daily, and customers could easily check availability, place orders, and choose delivery or pick-up. The admin dashboard tracked orders, payments, and customer details, while promo codes helped reward loyal customers. This solution gave Nikita's Curry Corner full control over operations and customer experience, allowing the business to grow independently of third-party platforms.

### Results

Increased direct food orders with targeted digital marketing driving traffic to their website.

Removed commission fees, ensuring full profit retention and pricing control.

Eliminate commission cuts, maximizing profits with complete pricing control.

Enhanced customer satisfaction with easy browsing, flexible delivery, and secure checkout.

Built customer loyalty by streamlining operations and offering personalized service.
