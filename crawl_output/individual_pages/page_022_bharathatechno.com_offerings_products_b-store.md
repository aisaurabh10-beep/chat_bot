# B-STORE - A Versatile E-Commerce Website to Manage and Grow Your Online Business

## B-Store

Full Stack E-Commerce Platform for Fashion - Beauty Brands

#### Product Overview

B-STORE is a scalable custom web app designed for fashion, beauty, and home décor brands. It gives businesses full control to design, manage, and grow their e-commerce website while delivering a seamless shopping experience. As a modern solution, B-STORE helps drive customer loyalty, streamline operations, and support sustainable growth.

### Tech Stack

##### Frontend

Next.js + TypeScript OR React.Js

##### Backend

Node.Js, Express.Js

##### Database

Mongo DB

##### Validation

Zod (Form validation)

##### Payments & Logistics

India: Razorpay, Global: Stripe, etc.

This stack allows the platform to load quickly, scale with order volume, and provide a smooth experience for both admin and end customers.

### Unique Selling Point

B-STORE allows sellers across any industry to operate an independent online store through a custom web app, providing full admin control without platform commissions. It includes all the tools needed to manage inventory, payments, returns, and customer satisfaction. Serving as a complete e-commerce website solution, B-STORE is designed to help businesses efficiently sell products online and scale their operations.

# Case Study: Trendy Threads By Riya

**Client:** Trendy Threads By Riya

**Industry:** Fashion & Lifestyle

**Use Case:** Branded eCommerce platform offering a wide range of clothing and accessories for women, including ethnic, fusion, and contemporary styles.

### Before the platform:

Before launching an independent platform, the business faced growing challenges on third-party marketplaces. Losses from damaged or used returns, manual order handling, and untrackable shipments led to operational inefficiencies. Without a dedicated website for selling products, there was no consistent brand identity or direct customer connection. Selling through platforms also limited trust and control, making it difficult to build a reliable, long-term online presence.

### After launching platform:

After launching a custom web app, the business gained full control over its operations, brand, and customer experience. Orders are now fully trackable, returns are efficiently managed, and manual processes have been replaced with a streamlined system. The dedicated e-commerce website establishes a consistent brand presence, builds customer trust, and enables direct engagement. With complete ownership over products, pricing, and promotions, the business is now well-positioned for sustainable growth and independence through an effective online platform.

### Results

Reduced return losses and manual inefficiencies.

Boost in customer satisfaction and retention.

Strong, independent brand identity.

Scalable digital operations now run entirely through the platform.
